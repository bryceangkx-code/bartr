Prompt C · Video Model The Transition — Digital to Physical Breakout 
START FRAME: A hand holding a dark smartphone in a relaxed 3/4 grip, low-angle view. Pure white background. The phone screen shows an open Instagram post featuring a pristine matte white cube with a black question mark (?) on its face, displayed inside the Instagram feed UI. The cube sits flat and still inside the screen. The hand is relaxed. Everything is still.

END FRAME: The same hand, same phone, same camera angle. The white question mark cube has physically burst through the phone screen, protruding halfway into the real world above the hand. Radial glass cracks spread from the exit point. Tiny glass shards float frozen in the air. The cube casts a real shadow on the hand. On the remaining visible screen area, a large red Instagram heart is visible and the like counter reads a large, blurred-motion number — climbing rapidly.

TRANSITION ANIMATION:
After a 0.5-second hold on the start frame, the cube begins to vibrate slightly — a subtle pre-tremor, like something trying to escape. Then, over 1.5 seconds, it pushes forward through the screen glass in a single clean motion: the glass fractures outward in a radial burst synchronized with the cube's emergence. Glass crack lines propagate across the screen surface at the moment of impact. Tiny glass shards eject outward and slowly float.

As the cube breaks through, the Instagram like counter begins rapidly incrementing — numbers blur upward. A red heart icon pulses and scales up briefly at the moment the cube exits.

The cube emerges fully and holds its position — half in, half out — for the final 1 second of the clip.

STYLE: Photorealistic. Pure white background throughout. Locked-off tripod shot — no camera movement, no handheld shake. The only motion is the cube's emergence, the glass fracture, and the like counter animation. ASMR-precise, satisfying physics. Think iPhone drop-test video meets Apple Keynote product reveal.

AUDIO SUGGESTION (optional): A crisp glass crack impact sound + satisfying "whoosh" as the cube emerges + rapid digital counter blip for the likes.

DURATION: 5–6 seconds
ASPECT RATIO: 16:9
QUALITY: Highest available fidelity, 24fps minimum
